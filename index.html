Who We Are

Chab Investment Company Limited is an organization focused on building, nurturing, exploring, and expanding the human & material resources for the improvement of society.

The company Limited was Instituted on the year 1991, registered by Pius Anyim (President of Nigerian senate and was Secretary to the Government of the Federation (SGF) during Goodluck Jonathan presidency).

Through our foray into Industrial Chemicals, Oil and other ventures, we have been of help to large number of people that come across us and have worked to build, enhance and explore people both young and old; making sure that God given destinies of most are realized.
